About Me

Hi my name is Grace, I'm an open minded communicator and compassionate leader. I love problem solving and am able to work in diverse workplace situations successfully.

Click here, Professional Webpage. This is another way to connect with me and see my work.

I became interested in STEM through programs like ChickTech Seattle and MESA. From there I joined another program called Girls Who Code which combined both my loves for business, and computer science. I plan on pursuing both as career!

On top of that, I am a part of the ambassador program at Year Up because I believe in bridging the opportunity divide for people of color.
